J.Demsky x VHTS collaboration kimono

1 product review $ 150.00

Graffiti artist J. Demsky’s work is heavily influenced from his childhood, growing up playing arcade games and watching VHS dubs of Japanese mecha and sci-fi movies. He operates simultaneously in nostalgia and futurism, the familiar and the never-before-seen, ‘90s retro and cutting-edge avant-garde. Now you can inspire awe and mesmerize your opponents in our exclusive, limited edition J. Demsky kimono collab. J. Demsky’s most recent designs focus on paradox - animation and movement in static compositions, seemingly impossible shapes that are simultaneously 2D and 3D.

You can deliver that same hypnotizing juxtaposition in our 425 GSM pearl weave and EVA foam lapel kimono, giving you ultimate comfort and flexibility while providing supreme durability to execute fluid and creative combinations with extreme accuracy.

The J. Demsky collab kimono’s accompanying pants are made from incredibly comfortable 10 oz cotton twill and have triangle knee pads built in to keep you protected as you put yourself through the toughest tests, redefining the limits of where your practices can go. Our limited edition J. Demsky kimono collab is AVAILABLE NOW in white. Boundaries were made to be broken.
